The quick brown fox jumped over the lazy brown dogs. The lazy brown dogs, infuriated, began to chase after the quick brown fox, but he got away.
There once was a brown fox
He lived in a little brown box.
One day he saw a few dogs sleeping
He went over to give them his greeting.
The dogs, startled by his presence, began to chase him
The fox ran, his escape was slim
He ran through the hay and lived to fox another day.
