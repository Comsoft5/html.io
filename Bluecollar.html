What are they?

Those who perform heavy physical labor are referred to as "blue-collar workers."
These workers are usually found in the manufacturing, construction, mining, agricultural, or maintenance industries.

Why are they called Blue-Collar Jobs

The phrase first appeared in the 1920s, when blue-collar workers were distinguished from white-collar office workers by donning darker-colored attire.
Probably because Blue-Collar workers got dirtier than white collar workers.
